How each edge type works

1UP — bet settles as a win if your team leads by 1 goal at ANY point during the match. Strictly more winning scenarios than 1X2.

2UP — same, but needs a 2-goal lead at some point. Harder to trigger but pays well.

Low Margin — when Sportybet's margin on a market is below 6% (vs typical 8%). Promotional pricing that gives back expected value.

Important: "+EV" means positive expected value over many bets. Single bets can still lose. Bankroll management matters.
